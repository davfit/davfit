Slappy




With his latest album "Warro Namorao", Slappy Av has established himself as one of the promises of the new wave of hyperpop, reaching a large audience in various parts of Spain. As the name of the album describes, its lyrics are full of the most explicit attachment and are accompanied by explosive productions. After his collaborations with artists such as Faxu or Funny Rage, he will present his album on his first date in the country's capital, the first letter of introduction for the entire musical phenomenon that awaits us in the coming years. He is currently finishing his next EP "FrigoLapi" with more varied and original rhythms than ever.

Midas Alonso




This rapper's style has nothing to do with Shakira's, but he is conquering the youngest and those who bet on more authentic sounds. And he has achieved it with 'Brixton', a song with dozens of sports references and whose video has become a true phenomenon for everything contained in its 4 minute and 45 second duration.

The video is recorded almost entirely in a single shot, while Midas Alonso is walking through an area full of almost empty terraces. The rapper doesn't hesitate and drank up to 5 alcoholic beverages one after the other, all of them in one gulp and almost without flinching, while he sings his song 'Brixton' and performs a most bizarre 'performance'.




Seccio




Andalusian rapper born in 2001, active since 2017. Having been influenced by flamenco all his life, he captures hard and difficult situations in his environment in modern rhythms but with classical touches, proud of his experiences but with the rage that having them instills. had to experiment.

Dirty Suc




Dirty suc is one of the most prominent young names in the new wave of urban music in Spain. He released his first album, 'Morriña', in 2021, in which he has vocal collaborations and musical productions by artists such as Beauty Pikete, Iaghost or Royce Rolo, among others. His releases reach exponential metrics on all digital platforms. Undoubtedly, one of the new Galician artists with the greatest projection.




Raul Clyde




Raul Clyde hit hard this year with the single 'Tuenti', one of those cuts that exploded and brought with it a great growth in his fan base. To end the year, he decides to please everyone who constantly asked him to and launch a new version of the hit, adding Saiko to the equation.

C Marí




C Marí is a 21-year-old artist from Valencia who likes to listen to and create sounds close to reggaeton, but he is also capable of breaking out of the mold without problems and approaching a more melodic and sung sound. He has several songs with Raul Clyde that have already hooked his followers (Equals + 500K) and some more to come out that will like even more. They are working on a joint project that promises to be an important step in their careers. It is also his debut on stage and it surely embodies that special attitude that characterizes him.
